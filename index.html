WHO WE ARE

Team Eco Vision is a multidisciplinary engineering team headquartered at Adamas University, dedicated to developing intelligent robotic systems, artificial intelligence solutions and impactful technologies for Healthcare, Agriculture, Defence and Smart Infrastructure. We believe innovation should solve real-world problems. Every prototype we build combines electronics, embedded systems, artificial intelligence, software engineering and mechanical design into practical engineering solutions.
