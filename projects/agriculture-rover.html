THE CHALLENGE

Why Modern Agriculture Needs Intelligent Robotics

Modern farming faces increasing challenges including labour shortages, inefficient irrigation, excessive fertilizer usage, inconsistent crop monitoring and rising operational costs. Traditional farming methods often rely on manual inspection, making large-scale precision agriculture difficult to implement efficiently.

OUR ENGINEERING APPROACH

Precision Farming through Artificial Intelligence

Team Eco Vision developed the Solar Powered Precision Agriculture Rover as a modular intelligent platform capable of autonomous navigation, environmental sensing, AI-assisted decision support and IoT-enabled monitoring. By integrating renewable energy, embedded electronics and artificial intelligence, the rover aims to improve productivity while reducing resource consumption and environmental impact.

MANUFACTURING WORKFLOW

Complete manufacturing process followed during the fabrication of the Solar Powered Precision Agriculture Rover.

The rover was developed using a structured engineering methodology, beginning with CAD modelling and dimensional planning, followed by material preparation, machining, drilling, sheet metal bending, component assembly, electronics integration, software deployment and final system testing. This workflow ensured systematic fabrication and simplified future maintenance and upgrades.

WORKSHOP DEVELOPMENT LOG

Real workshop activities carried out during the fabrication and assembly of the Agriculture Rover.

STEP 01

Precision Drilling

STEP 02

Sheet Metal Bending

STEP 03
